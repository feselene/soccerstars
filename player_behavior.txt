Offensive players will try to kick the ball into the goal and try to kick the other players away from the goal. 
Defensive players will focus on keeping their players in front of the goal and put their players between the goal and the ball. 

AI Requirements: Can shoot ball into goal and do multiple collisions. Don't leave the goal wide open. 

Development Environment: Python
Emulator: Bluestacks
Dependencies: Python, OpenCV, PyTorch, NumPy, TensorFlow (optional), and YOLO.
